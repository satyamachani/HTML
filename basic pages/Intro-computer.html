`ASCII' American Standard Code for Information Interchanged.

`ENIAC' Electronic Numeric Integrator and Calculator Is the first electronic machine
	  introduced in 1946 by J.Presper Eckert and John W.Mauchly Proffesors at Moore School of 
	  University of Pennsylvania and was called ENIAC. This computer was built to help 
	  U.S.Army to prepare Artillery Trajectory tables.There were 6000 switches which set to 
	  solve problems manually.It was a large machine(30 ft. X 50 ft) containing 18,000vaccum
	  tubes,70,000 resisters, 10,000 capacitors and had a small memory for storing data.It 
	  could process 300 calculation per second. 
	
`BINAC'(Binary Automatic Computer) was designed in 1949 and could receive data from a 
	typewriter,keyboard or from a magnetic tape.

`UNIVAC'(Universal Automatic Computer)made in 1951 used 1200 foot reels of metal tapes to
 	record data for processing.
`IBM' International Business Machines.

GENERATION: Generation means improvement in the computer technology.Generation are mainly 
	    divided into 5 types.

(1) First Generation: 1950's In this genaration computer are developed with Vaccum tubes.This 
	computer are very large in size with slow operating speed. 
(2) Second Generation: 1960's Inthis generation computers are developed with transistors with
	the replacement of Vaccum Tubes.
(3) Third Generation: 1970's Inthis generation IntelCorporations introduced IC Chips With the 
	Replacement of Resisters. (Integrated Circuits)
(4) Fourth Generation: 1980's Inthis generation computer are developed by the replacement of IC
	chips with LSIC chips.
(5) Fifth Generation: 1990's Inthis generation computer are developed by the replacement of LSIC
	with VLSIC chips. This computer are very small in size can store more information than 
	the previous generation with high operating speed and computing capacity.
