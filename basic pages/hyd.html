colconda still boasts of mounted cannons, four drawbags,eight gateways, and majestic halls, magazeines, stables etc,. the outermost enclosure is called fateh darwaza meaning victory gate, after aurangzeb's army marched successfully through this gate. At fatesh darwaza one can witness fantastic acoustical effects, which is one among the many famous engineering marvels at golconda. clapping your hand at a certain point near he dome entrance reverberates which is heard clearly at the hill top pavilion, almost one kilometer away
